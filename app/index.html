Lessson 1

Ableton live Overview

In this lesson you will getfamiliar with Live’s interface, two different views, browser, timeline, tracks, buttons, loops etc. You will get to know everything by practical tasks and this will put you in the comfort zone right away. After that you’ll be ready to go deeper and make a drum beat.

Lessson 2

Making beats in Ableton Live

Here you will discover how to make beats from scratch in Ableton Live. You will learn different techniques and work in the Arrangement mode, use one shot samples and MIDI editor, learn editing and timeline navigation.

Lessson 3

Recording Audio/MIDI and Devices

In this lesson you will learn the fundamentals on how to edit and Warp audio clips, use MIDI instruments, how to make patterns, basic arrangement and discover some audio effects. We will also work with some basic mixing tools, so you could shape your beats.

Lessson 4

Basic Music Theory Warp / Envelopes

We will go through some basic music theory, so you would understand how to build chords and basslines. You will absorb all the information by practical tasks and examples. You will also learn some Warp and Envelope techniques in more detail.

Lessson 5

Sampling

You will learn what is sampling and how to make a sample based beat. You will also learn how to make a bass-line and discover some stereo effects.

Lessson 6

Synthesizers and Basic Synthesis

You will learn what is sampling and how to make a sample based beat. You will also learn how to make a bass-line and discover some stereo effects.

Lessson 7

Audio Effects and Controllers

In this lesson you will learn how to apply audio effects, what they do and how to set them right. We will make a beat and figure it all out in practice.

Lessson 8

Mixing

You will learn the basic mixing techniques including Leveling, Panning, Equalization, Compression, FX’s etc. I will share the most important tips and tricks and mix a track from start to finish ,so you could then mix your own one.

Lessson 9

Arrangement and Song Structure

In this lesson you will learn how to turn your loops into complete tracks by implementing these arrangement techniques. You will also get to know how to make your songs more interesting and professional.

Lessson 10

Live Set and Session View

Here you will learn how to prepare your track for a live performance and discover the Session View in more detail. I will also share some cool tips and tricks on how to boost your live set.
